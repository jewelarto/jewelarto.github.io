Kelli Carter's Journey to Success: How She Overcame Adversity and Achieved Her Dreams!

Kelli Carter is an American model, actress, and dancer. She was born in Florida and raised in the United Kingdom.

She has been involved in the entertainment industry for over ten years, appearing in a variety of television shows, films and commercials. In 2019, she made her feature film debut in the drama “The Art of Racing in the Rain”. Kelli began her career as a dancer at a young age. She trained for several years in ballet, jazz and hip-hop dance styles before making her first foray into film and television. Her first role was a small part in the ABC Family show “Greek”. Since then, she has appeared on shows such as “Glee”, “Modern Family” and “Supergirl”. She also had a recurring role on the hit Netflix series “13 Reasons Why” as Courtney Crimson. Kelli has also appeared in several music videos for artists such as Justin Bieber, Maroon 5 and Zara Larsson. Her most recent endeavor was appearing in one of Justin Bieber’s music videos for his single “Yummy". Kelli has also begun to make her mark as an actress with roles in films such as “The Art of Racing in the Rain" and "The Neighbor". In addition to acting and dancing, Kelli is also an accomplished model who has appeared on numerous magazine covers including Vogue India, Cosmopolitan UK and Glamour Mexico. In 2019 she was named one of Maxim Magazine's Hot 100 Women of 2019. Kelli is also highly active on social media where she shares her thoughts on beauty, fashion and lifestyle with over two million followers on Instagram alone! With so much talent packed into one person it's no wonder that Kelli is considered one of the hottest rising stars to watch out for!
