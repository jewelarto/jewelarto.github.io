The 10 Best Fashion Bloggers in Utah You Need to Follow Right Now!

Utah is home to some of the most talented fashion bloggers in the United States. These incredible bloggers have grown their following and have become highly sought-after for their stylish looks and advice.

From street style to luxury fashion, these bloggers have it all! The first blog we’d like to highlight is Haute & Honey. Run by Sarah Curran, she has been a fashion blogger since 2012. She offers readers a look into what she’s wearing as well as her own personal style thoughts and tips. From fashion events to product reviews, Sarah provides readers with an honest look into what she likes and dislikes about the latest trends. Next is Salt City Style, which is run by Amber Tingey. Amber has been blogging since 2013, and her blog features a variety of looks from classic glamour to edgy street style. She also likes to showcase local businesses, giving readers an inside look into Utah’s fashion scene. Kate Kearney runs the blog Hello Fashion Blog, which provides readers with insight on how to dress for different occasions. Kate has been blogging since 2015 and loves creating affordable yet stylish looks. She also offers tips on how to make the most out of your wardrobe without overspending on expensive items. Kelsey Petersen runs the blog Sincerely Kels and focuses on creating timeless looks that are effortless yet sophisticated. Kelsey has been blogging since 2011 and loves showcasing her own personal style as well as talking about current trends in an informative way that makes them accessible for everyone. Finally we have Laura Pambianchi who runs Grandma Glamourous which offers insight on how to stay stylish at any age! Laura has been blogging since 2017 and often showcases vintage-inspired looks that are timeless yet fashionable. Not only does she provide advice on what pieces are worth investing in but she also gives tips on how to maintain clothes so they stay looking new for longer periods of time! These five amazing Utah based fashion bloggers provide great inspiration for both men and women living in or visiting Utah! If you’re looking for some fresh takes on current trends or just looking for a few pieces to update your wardrobe then these are definitely the bloggers you should check out!
