Discover the Unique Style of Bridge Piercing Jewelry!

Bridge piercings are a unique and aesthetically pleasing type of facial piercing. It is a single piercing located between the eyes, on the bridge of the nose, that is generally used to hang jewelry such as rings, studs, and barbells.

The piercing itself is relatively simple but choosing the right jewelry can be tricky. In this article, we will discuss some of the key considerations when selecting bridge piercing jewelry. First, it’s important to consider what type of material you want your jewelry to be made out of. Common materials include titanium, stainless steel, gold, silver and acrylic. Some materials may be more comfortable to wear than others or may better suit your personal style preferences. Second, consider the size and shape of the bridge jewelry. You’ll want to make sure it fits comfortably between your eyes and that it isn’t too heavy for your nose. Typically for a bridge piercing you would use a curved barbell or circular ring with an inner diameter no larger than 8mm in order to avoid any irritation or pressure on the skin. Third, think about whether you would prefer light or dark colored jewelry for your bridge piercing. Jewelry with lighter colors can draw attention to your face and look more subtle while darker colors can make your piercings stand out more prominently in comparison with other facial features. Finally, consider whether you would prefer gemstones or charms as part of your bridge piercing jewelry. Gemstone studs add sparkle and color while charms add personality and charm! Adding decorative elements such as these are definitely optional but they can really personalize your look and make it unique to you! These are just some of the factors you should consider when selecting bridge piercing jewelry but ultimately it is up to you what you decide on! Remember that whatever type of jewelry you choose should not only fit comfortably but also reflect your own individual style!
