The Latest in French Fashion: What to Wear in 2021!

France is known worldwide as a fashion capital, with French style setting the trend for many of the global fashion trends. French fashion has always been a major influence on the world’s wardrobe, from the classic white shirt and striped Breton top to chic little black dresses and statement accessories.

There is a certain je ne sais quoi when it comes to French style that is both timeless and modern – an effortless sophistication that has become synonymous with Parisian chic. The French have long had an eye for detail when it comes to fashion — from the cut of a suit jacket to the fit of a dress — and this attention to detail has made them one of the most influential countries in terms of setting global trends. The Paris Fashion Week is one of the world’s biggest events, launching new collections and showcasing some of the best designers in France. French style can be summed up as “elegance with a twist” – combining classic pieces with unexpected elements to create something unique and timeless. French women have their own unique take on fashion, prioritizing quality over quantity when it comes to buying clothes. A few classic staples are at the heart of every wardrobe, such as tailored trousers or skirts, crisp white shirts, blazers, heels or flats and chic accessories like scarves or jewelry. While trends come and go in France just like anywhere else, it’s clear that there is something special about French style that will continue to influence global fashion for years to come. From timeless elegance to modern sophistication – whatever your style there’s something for everyone in France!
