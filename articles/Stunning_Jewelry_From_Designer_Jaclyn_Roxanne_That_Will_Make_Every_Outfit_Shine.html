Stunning Jewelry From Designer Jaclyn Roxanne That Will Make Every Outfit Shine!

Jaclyn Roxanne Jewelry is a small, independent, U.S.-based jewelry company that creates beautiful and unique pieces inspired by nature. Founded in 2014 by Jaclyn Malone, Jaclyn Roxanne Jewelry strives to create meaningful and timeless pieces that are both wearable and beautiful.

The brand’s signature style draws heavily from nature’s beauty—featuring organic elements like feathers, leaves, and stones—and this attention to detail sets the brand apart from other jewelry companies. Jaclyn Roxanne Jewelry offers a range of necklaces, bracelets, rings, and earrings for both men and women. The pieces are made with different types of metal like gold-filled brass and sterling silver as well as different types of stones including turquoise, labradorite, and jade. Each piece is handcrafted with care by artisans in their studio in the Midwest United States. On top of that the company offers free repairs on all of their products if they become damaged over time or if any part needs replacing. The company also works to ensure that its products are ethically sourced. All of their stones are sourced from suppliers who have been vetted for responsible practices when it comes to environmental protection as well as social impact. In addition to this they also maintain fair trade relationships with suppliers around the world who help provide them with materials for their pieces. Jaclyn Roxanne Jewelry is dedicated to creating meaningful pieces that evoke a sense of connection between its wearers and nature itself. Through their designs they strive to remind us all how much beauty there is in the world around us even during times when we may feel disconnected or overwhelmed. The thoughtfulness behind each piece makes them perfect gifts for yourself or someone you care about—so why not check out what they have to offer today?
