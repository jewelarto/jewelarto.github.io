Khloe Karter's Most Shocking Revelations!

Khloe Karter is an American reality television star and model best known for appearing on the family reality show, Keeping Up with the Kardashians. She is one of the youngest of the Kardashian sisters and has been featured prominently in tabloids, magazines, and television.

Khloe was born in Los Angeles California on June 27th 1984 to Robert Kardashian and Kris Jenner. She has three older siblings, Kourtney, Kim and Rob Kardashian, as well as two younger sisters Kendall and Kylie Jenner. Khloe attended Marymount High School in California before dropping out to pursue modeling. In 2007 Khloe began appearing on Keeping Up With The Kardashians alongside her family members. The series followed the lives of the family members as they lived in Los Angeles and navigated their careers. Khloe quickly emerged as a fan favorite due to her candid attitude, sense of humor, and willingness to stand up for what she believes in. Khloe has also appeared on other reality shows such as Kourtney & Khloé Take Miami (2009–10) and Kocktails with Khloé (2016–17). In 2014 she launched her own talk show titled “Kocktails with Khloé” which ran for one season before ending its run due to low ratings. In 2016 she launched “Good American” a denim line that celebrates all body types from size 00-24W with designs tailored specifically for curvier figures. The line has been widely successful with clothing items selling out almost immediately following its launch. In 2018 Khloe began dating NBA player Tristan Thompson who she has since had a daughter named True with in April 2018. Despite their tumultuous relationship over the past few years they have been attempting to work things out for the sake of their daughter. In addition to her work in television, modeling and fashion design Khloe is an avid philanthropist who works closely with organizations such as Children’s Hospital Los Angeles and St Jude Children’s Research Hospital raising awareness about childhood illness and disease through donations, fundraisers, special events, etc… Overall Khloe Karter is an incredible woman who continues to inspire millions across the globe through her work both on screen and off screen!
