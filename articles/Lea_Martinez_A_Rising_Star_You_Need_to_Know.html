Lea Martinez: A Rising Star You Need to Know!

Lea Martinez is an accomplished singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist from Spain. She is known for her smooth vocals and unique sound, which mixes traditional folk with modern pop.

She has released four albums, two EPs, and a handful of singles since her self-titled debut in 2014. Born and raised in Madrid, Martinez began singing early on and soon developed an interest in both music theory and composition. She studied classical guitar at a local conservatory before eventually deciding to pursue a career in music. Her first two albums showcased her love of folk music with her unique take on traditional Spanish sounds. With her third album, she began to incorporate elements of jazz, soul, and pop into her sound. The result was a much more contemporary feel that resonated with listeners around the world. Her songwriting style reflects her deep understanding of the history and culture of Spain, as well as its current trends and issues. Her lyrics are poetic yet straightforward; often focusing on themes of love, identity, family dynamics, and social justice. Furthermore, she unifies these topics together with a strong message of hope for the future. From festivals to clubs to sold out shows at world-renowned venues such as the Royal Albert Hall in London, Martinez has consistently performed live during her career thus far. She also regularly collaborates with other artists from around the world including renowned jazz pianist Chano Domínguez or Spanish producer Tato Latorre who has worked alongside Glastonbury favourite Flamingods or Bombay Bicycle Club’s Jamie MacColl to name a few! Martinez’s latest release is the EP “La Vida” which dropped in 2019 with lead single ‘Nada Más’ receiving wide critical acclaim throughout Europe. The EP features six tracks that showcase Martinez’s signature smooth vocals paired with acoustic/electronic instrumentation as well as several guest musicians providing additional assistance on guitar licks or backing vocals making this one of her most diverse releases yet! Lea Martinez is an artist that has consistently grown throughout her career whilst staying true to herself musically speaking without ever straying too far away from where she started off way back when… With each release she continues to strive for excellence providing listeners from around the globe with timeless music that can make any day just that little bit brighter!
