See How Fans Are Paying Tribute to Michael Afton With Incredible Artwork!

Michael Afton, otherwise known as Mike, is a major antagonist in the horror game series Five Nights at Freddy's. The character has gained immense popularity among fans of the series, and some talented artists have created beautiful fan art of him.

Michael Afton was first introduced in Five Nights at Freddy's 3. In the game, he is a security guard who works the night shift at a pizzeria that contains animatronic characters. He is searching for his missing sister, Elizabeth Afton, and to do this, he must survive five nights at the pizzeria while being pursued by scary animatronics. His identity is revealed during the sixth night when it is discovered that he is actually the son of William Afton, a serial killer responsible for several murders involving the animatronics. Since his introduction in Five Nights at Freddy's 3, Michael Afton has become an incredibly popular character among fans of the series. Many fan artists have taken up the challenge to bring him to life with their artwork. On DeviantArt and other websites dedicated to fan art, there are countless versions of Michael Afton to be found. Some depict him as a heroic figure fighting against evil forces; others show him as a scared young man desperately seeking his lost sister amidst terror and danger; still others represent him as a tragic villain doomed by his own guilt and mistakes.
