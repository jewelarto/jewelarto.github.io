Unlock the Secrets Behind Jewel's 'You Were Meant for Me' Lyrics!

Jewel’s “You Were Meant for Me” is a heart-warming love song about a person who believes that their significant other was made specifically for them. The lyrics evoke feelings of longing, love, and appreciation, making it the perfect ode to a special someone.

The song begins with Jewel’s sweet and almost nostalgic vocals singing: “I hear the clock, it's 6 am/I feel so far from where I've been/I got my eggs and my pancakes too/The morning air is all I need to know.” This verse paints a vivid image of Jewel waking up in the early morning hours thinking about her lover and what they mean to her. Despite being apart, Jewel finds comfort in the shared experiences they have had together. The chorus of “You Were Meant for Me” also carries this message of love and longing: “You were meant for me/And I was meant for you/No matter what we do, it keeps coming back to/ You were meant for me/And I was meant for you.” These lyrics are an affirmation that no matter what life throws at them, their connection will always remain strong. The bridge of the song elaborates on this message by emphasizing Jewel's appreciation for her partner: “All of my dreams come true when I'm near you/Though life can be cruel, you make it look beautiful/Oh baby when you smile at me my heart stops beating just like a drum in time with you.” Here we see Jewel expressing gratitude for all of the joy that her partner brings into her life - even during difficult times. Overall, Jewel’s “You Were Meant for Me” is an emotionally charged love song about two people who were brought together by destiny. Its lyrics remind us to appreciate our partners and never take them for granted - no matter how long or short our time together may be.
