The Frustrating Reality of Parking at Fashion Show Mall

Fashion Show Mall Parking is the ideal solution for anyone looking to shop, dine, or just enjoy the Las Vegas Strip. Located on the north side of Las Vegas Boulevard, Fashion Show Mall offers easy access to both Las Vegas Boulevard and the I-15 freeway.

With a total of 11,000 parking spaces, there is plenty of space to accommodate your shopping needs. The Fashion Show Mall offers two main parking areas: the main surface lot and the five-story parking garage. The main surface lot is located directly in front of the mall and has ample space for cars, buses and oversized vehicles. The five-story parking garage is located just north of the main lot and can be accessed from any level. In addition to this, there are also three valet lots available for those who do not wish to park themselves. The mall also offers a paid parking system which allows shoppers to pay for their time spent at the mall using a debit or credit card at any of its automated machines located throughout both lots. In addition to this, shoppers can use their phones or an app to pay with their credit or debit cards. The cost for paid parking varies depending on length of stay and time of day but typically ranges from $1-$3 per hour. The Fashion Show Mall also offers several convenient services that make it easier for visitors to get around while shopping or dining at the mall. These services include electric scooters available for rent from designated kiosks, shuttle services from nearby hotels, and a complimentary valet service located near both entrances that allows visitors to drop off their vehicles in a secure location before heading inside the mall. Overall, Fashion Show Mall Parking provides customers with an easy and affordable way to take in all that the mall has to offer without having to worry about finding a place to park when they arrive. With plenty of space available and its convenient services like valet parking, electric scooter rentals and shuttle services from nearby hotels, shoppers are sure to have an enjoyable experience while they shop around one of Las Vegas’ premier shopping destinations.
