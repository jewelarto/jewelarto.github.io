Find Out the Secret to Tracking Your Fashion Nova Order - Uncover the Answers Here!

Fashion Nova is one of the fastest growing online fashion retailers, offering an expansive selection of trendy and stylish apparel for men, women, and children. Whether you’re shopping for the latest trends or looking to build a timeless wardrobe, Fashion Nova has something to suit any aesthetic.

Though shopping with Fashion Nova is easy, understanding your order status can be a bit more complicated. To help you navigate this process, we’ve put together a comprehensive guide to tracking your Fashion Nova order. First things first – as soon as you place an order at Fashion Nova, you’ll receive an email confirming your purchase. This email will include your order number and estimated arrival date. You can also log in to your account at www.fashionnova.com/order-status to view all the details about your order. Once your package leaves our fulfillment center, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information for that specific order. If you do not receive the shipping confirmation email within 72 hours of placing your order (excluding weekends and holidays), please contact us directly at customerservice@fashionnova.com so that we can look into it for you! You can also track your package directly from the carrier’s website using the tracking number provided in the shipping confirmation email. For US orders shipped via FedEx or USPS, click on "Track Package" in the email we sent to view the progress of your shipment, or go directly to www.fedex.com or www.usps.com and enter the tracking number there.. If you placed an international order outside of US territories that was shipped via FedEx, DHL Express or Aramex Express USA please use their respective websites to track: www.fedex.com , www2.dhl-usa/en/express/tracking , www1aramexusa/track-shipments/. Once delivered, it's important that you inspect all items upon delivery before disposing of any packaging material as this may be needed later if an issue arises with one or more items from the shipment (e .g., damaged items). It's also important that customers retain their original invoice/packing slip for their records as well as proof of purchase should a return be necessary due to an item defect or exchange request due to size issues.. We hope this guide has helped make understanding your Fashion Nova order status easier!
